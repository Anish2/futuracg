  • Instate Tuition Fees

  • Settle Permanently in The United States of America With Family

  • Freedom to Live, Work, Study anywhere in the USA

  • No English Language skill or business experience required

  • Created as a “Job creation” and foreign Investment program

BENEFITS OF USA GREEN CARD:

  • Permanent Residency

  • The first and only step towards citizenship

  • Freedom to work and live anywhere in the USA

  • Ability to own and operate business anywhere in the USA

  • Instate tuitions for education

  • Opportunity for Access to high quality Healthcare

PATH TO USA GREEN CARD:

  • Subscription & Escrow Agreement, Funds & Docs. Requirement

  • Prepare I-526 for Green Card

  • I-526 completed, Filed & Submitted at USCIS

  • USCIS issues a receipt of I-526 – Conditional Green Card

  • Conditional Green Card Issued

  • Go to USA Consulate for Visa Stamp

  • Fly to America to collect Conditional Green Card

  • File I-829 to file for your permanent Green Card

  • Get Permanent Green Card

  • $500,000 returned

  • Filing of American Citizenship
